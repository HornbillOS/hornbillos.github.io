What is HornbillOS?

HornbillOS is an AOSP & LineageOS based ROM, with some own open source apps (Gallery, Notes, etc.). Hornbill contains almost all AOSP and Pixel goodies.

Why does this exist?

When we look at the Custom ROM world, there are tons with lots of customisation and stuffs. What we intend to do is give users the ROM which satisfies your daily needs and helps your device to last long.

What to expect from us?

We intend to give a stable ROM with OTA support and everything work out of box.
